Naturosynth Live

this is your sign to go to local arts festivals so you can meet people who have the same weird interests as you. i met someone who has the same exact interests as me and we ended up essentially creating this livecoding hub in D.C.

As someone really inspired by music I found myself drawn to creating live visuals for them to potentially play at their shows. In addition to live performance artwork, I also made a couple of flyers and 3d designed their logo to allow some design room for other merch ideas such as stickers and patches.
