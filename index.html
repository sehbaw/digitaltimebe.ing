"Progress depends on changing the world to fit us. Not the other way around." welcome to another extension of my digital footprint :p here I am providing some information about my creative endeavors which I am trying to do more of. as someone who has always been interested in creative technology and design I recently started to have audacity to pursue things and move forward as someone who creates rather than just consumes! The main motif I would love to convey in my work is recontextualization. I feel as if a lot of artists do this subconsciously as they work metaphors into their work and don't idealize technology. A machine is a machine, a human is a human. Machines can be broken down and rebuilt. A human can't. I would like to showcase alternatives to predetermined understandings of words and ideas.
